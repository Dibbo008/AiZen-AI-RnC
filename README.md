# AiZen – AI-Powered Resume & Cover Letter Builder

AiZen is an intelligent career tool crafted with a futuristic dark aesthetic and a professional edge. Designed using React.js and powered by the Gemini 2.5 Flash API, AiZen lets users build ATS-optimized resumes and compelling cover letters in minutes. Deployed seamlessly via Netlify, AiZen features a sleek, modern interface with dark/light mode support for an immersive career-building experience.

## Features

- *AI-Generated Professional Summary*
Let AiZen craft a powerful 3–4 sentence professional bio based on your name, job title, years of experience, and key strengths — fully editable after generation.
- *Work Experience Enhancer*
Input your raw job description and AiZen rewrites it into 4–5 polished, ATS-friendly bullet points using strong action verbs and measurable impact.
- *AI Skills Suggester*
Enter your job title and get a categorized list of relevant hard skills, tools, and soft skills — click to instantly add or remove them from your resume.
- *3 Live Resume Templates*
Switch between Classic, Modern, and Minimal templates with a live preview that updates in real time as you fill in your details.
- *Cover Letter Generator*
Generate a full, professional cover letter tailored to a specific job title and company — editable, copyable, and downloadable as PDF.
- *PDF Export*
Download your resume or cover letter as a print-ready A4 PDF in one click — no formatting lost.
- *Dark / Light Mode Toggle*
Switch between a deep dark theme and a clean light theme at any time from the navbar.
- *Fully Responsive Design*
AiZen works beautifully across mobile phones, tablets (iPad), and desktop — with a dedicated Edit Form / Preview & PDF toggle for smaller screens.

## Future Enhancements

- *ATS Score Checker*
Integrate an AI-powered ATS scoring system that rates how well your resume matches a specific job description and suggests improvements.
- *Chat Memory & Resume History*
Allow users to save multiple resume versions locally and revisit or edit previous drafts without starting from scratch.
- *LinkedIn Import*
Auto-fill resume sections by importing directly from a LinkedIn profile URL, saving manual data entry time.
